# 7 Core Value Propositions by Stakeholder Group for Hummingbird MVP

## FOR PARENTS
(Based on Personas 1–7: Overloaded Working Parent, Co-Parenting Navigator, Attached Caregiver, Anxious First-Time Parent, The Multi-child Juggler, The Remote/Hybrid Parent, The Traveling Professional Parent)

1.  **"Feel closer to your child's day, no matter where you are."** Personalized Content Feed AND a Personalized Al-generated photo and video TikTok Style digests give you meaningful, emotionally rich updates that make you feel like you were there - even on your busiest days. Spotify wrapped for your kids day (20-seconds of content, video, text, music, that gives a parent everything you need/want to know about that day and the coming days in a short-form format) video example
    *   Includes what your kid learned, activities, behavioral etc.
    *   Pictures
    *   School updates
    *   Class-specific updates
    *   Requests of the parents (i.e. bring diapers, bring swimsuit etc.)
2.  **"Skip the guilt trip."** Prioritized Notifications Page, with sorting etc.
    No more late-night scrolling through folders or missed posts - Hummingbird delivers everything you need to know in one place, automatically.
3.  **"Share magical moments with co-parents easily.”** Separate logins for co-parents
    Both caregivers get the same beautifully packaged updates, so everyone stays in the loop, even when parenting from different places.
    *   All communications are available across all parents of the student.
4.  **"Know what to ask your child after school.”** After-school conversation starters
    Our smart recaps offer conversation starters based on the day's highlights so you can engage meaningfully at dinner, even if you didn't see the whole day.
    *   Does this live in the same place as the 20-second digest.
        *   Yes
    *   Narrate this to you
5.  **"Choose how often you get updates.”** Notification preferences
    Detailed notification preferences, so the information comes when you want it, the way you want it: daily, weekly, or just the highlights - you control the volume so it fits your lifestyle without overwhelm.
    *   Is this a sub-feature of prioritized notifications page?
        *   Yes
    *   Smart Priority/urgency level of notifications
6.  **"Always feel confident in your child's care."**
    With Al-supported consistency, updates are equally rich across every classroom - no more wondering why one class shares more than another.
    *   Consistent communication/interaction across the class.
7.  **"The modern parenting tool you didn't know you needed.”** Mobile app
    A gorgeous, mobile-first experience built for real families, finally bringing childcare tech into the 21st century.
    *   One stop - parents interact with the school via one modality/channel. (no more emails, calls, multiple apps etc.

## FOR TEACHERS
(Based on Personas: Passionate Overcommunicator, Child-Focused Minimalist, ESL Educator, Overwhelmed Newcomer)

1.  **"Teach more. Type less.”** Al-generated captions on photos
    Smart capture tools + Al-generated captions = less time documenting, more time connecting with kids.
2.  **"Your classroom's beauty, shared effortlessly."** Automatic tagging of children for posts
    One tap to capture the magic. Hummingbird auto-tags children and selects the best shots for the end-of-day video digest, personalized by the child and automatically delivered to their parents.
3.  **"No more pressure to write perfect posts.”** post/message writing assistant
    Whether you're ESL or just tired, Hummingbird handles the grammar, tone, and warmth, aligned with the pedagogy of your school.
4.  **"Protect your flow state."** voice activated note taking
    No switching apps mid-moment. Hummingbird lives where the action is; fast, intuitive, voice activated and invisible when you need it to be.
5.  **"Be celebrated, not burned out.”** insights on parent engagement
    We recognize your voice, your style, your work - and help admins do the same with engagement insights and smart feedback tools.
6.  **“Less time explaining, more time inspiring.”** recommended responses to parent questions
    Auto-digests and our front desk bot help reduce repetitive Q&A with parents. Let the updates speak for themselves.
7.  **"Designed to support you, not replace you.”** train models on the teacher's writing style
    Every tool is built with teacher voice and well-being in mind. You're the hero, Hummingbird is your sidekick. We'll free up your mental load so you can be more engaged with the kids and creative in your profession!

## FOR SCHOOL ADMINS/DIRECTORS
(Based on Admin + School Owner Persona: Growth-Focused Owner/Director)

1.  **"One app to rule them all."** all school tools live in one app.
    Ditch the Frankenstein tech stack. Hummingbird consolidates parent engagement, documentation, and communication into one beautiful, branded experience. Your school's intuitive branded app becomes the place your parents go for all things school engagement.
2.  **"Consistent, high-quality updates across all classrooms.”** tools to set how frequent ai-created teacher communications are?
    No more gaps between 'superstar' teachers and those who struggle with updates. Hummingbird raises the baseline.
3.  **"Delight parents. Reduce complaints."** post/message writing assistant
    Automated personalized daily digests, centralized announcements, and fewer missed moments make your school stand out - and parents feel seen.
4.  **"Cut teacher turnover by removing their biggest pain point."** post/message writing assistant and tagging of students
    Reduce burnout and increase retention by automating documentation and freeing teachers to focus on children.
5.  **"Real data to support staff and spot trends.”** insights on parent engagement
    Monitor classroom communication levels, parent engagement, and even Al-suggested coaching moments.
6.  **"Look like the innovative school you are."** white-labeling of app
    Hummingbird is white-labeled to reflect your brand. You own the magic — we just make it easy.
7.  **"A system your team will actually use."**
    Built for ECE from day one, with Al woven in naturally — not bolted on. Fast onboarding, simple UI, and real support.
