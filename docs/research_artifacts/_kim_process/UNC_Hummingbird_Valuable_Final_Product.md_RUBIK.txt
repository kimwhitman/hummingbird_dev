Executive Summary:
This document defines Hummingbird's core values and articulates the vision for its "Valuable Final Product." The core values are Human Connection, Teacher Liberation, Family Empowerment, Authentic Documentation, and Educational Excellence. The product aims to transform educational communities by enhancing relationships, freeing teachers from administrative burdens, empowering parents, and elevating the educational experience through seamless school-to-home connections. The vision for the final product describes a state where teachers are liberated and recognized, parents feel deeply connected and empowered, administrators lead with confidence, schools are market leaders with streamlined operations and strong reputations, and children benefit from celebrated milestones and documented journeys. The overarching philosophy is that technology should enhance, not replace, human connections, turning documentation into meaningful stories.

Core Values:
1.  Human Connection: Technology enhances meaningful relationships.
2.  Teacher Liberation: Freeing educators for presence and play over paperwork.
3.  Family Empowerment: Transforming parents into active partners.
4.  Authentic Documentation: Capturing genuine moments and stories.
5.  Educational Excellence: Elevating the experience through seamless school-home connection.

Valuable Final Product Vision:
Hummingbird aims to create highly engaged, confident, and empowered educational communities characterized by:
-   **Teachers:** Liberated from burdens, reconnected with passion, recognized for creating magical moments.
-   **Parents:** Deeply connected to child's experiences, equipped for home learning, transformed into school advocates.
-   **Administrators:** Leading with data-driven insights, standardized excellence, and a distinctive brand identity.
-   **Schools:** Market leaders with streamlined operations, reduced turnover, waitlists, and innovative parent engagement.
-   **Children:** Benefiting from seamless school-to-home experiences, celebrated milestones, and intentional documentation.

Core Philosophy:
Technology should enhance human connections rather than replace them, turning everyday documentation into meaningful stories that build trust, loyalty, and educational excellence.
